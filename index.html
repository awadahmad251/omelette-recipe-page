Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic Omelette combines beaten eggs
cooked for perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meat.

Preparation Time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

    • Ingredients

    • 2-3 large Eggs
    • Salt,to taste
    • Pepper,to tase
    • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
    • Optional fillings: cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meat,herbs.

    Instructions

    1. Beat the eggs: Crack eggs into a bowl, add a pinch of salt and pepper, and whisk until smooth.
    2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick pan over medium heat and melt a small knob of butter.
    3. Cook the omelette: Pour the eggs in, tilting the pan so they spread evenly.
    4. Add fillings (optional): Sprinkle cheese, vegetables, or cooked meat over the eggs.
    5. Fold and serve: Gently fold the omelette in half and slide it onto a plate.
    6. Enjoy: Serve hot with toast, salad, or your favorite sides.
